# Privacy Policy for VeriScript

**Last updated: May 28, 2025**

VeriScript ("we", "us", or "our") operates the VeriScript mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use VeriScript.

By downloading or using VeriScript, you agree to the collection and use of information as described in this policy.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

### Information you provide directly
- **Name and email address** — collected when you create a VeriScript account
- **Documents you upload** — any files (PDF, DOCX, TXT) you submit for plagiarism scanning, file conversion, OCR scanning, or translation
- **Text input** — text you type or paste directly into the Translator or OCR result screen
- **Referral codes** — if you enter or share a referral code

### Information collected automatically
- **Usage data** — which features you use, how often, and timestamps of activity
- **Device information** — device model, operating system version, and app version
- **Crash and error reports** — technical data about app errors sent to our monitoring service
- **Connectivity data** — whether you are online or offline when using the app

### Information we do NOT collect
- We do not collect your location
- We do not access your camera roll or files beyond what you explicitly select and upload
- We do not collect payment card details — all payments are handled entirely by Google Play

---

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

- Create and manage your VeriScript account
- Process your documents through our third-party service partners (plagiarism detection, file conversion, translation, OCR)
- Display your usage quota and history within the app
- Send you notifications when document processing is complete
- Provide offline access to your previously processed documents
- Operate the referral programme and award bonus scans
- Monitor app performance and fix technical issues
- Improve existing features and develop new ones
- Respond to your support requests sent to desolate1250@gmail.com

We do not use your information for advertising. We do not sell your information to any third party.

---

## 3. Documents You Upload

Your uploaded documents are stored securely on Supabase infrastructure. They are used solely to provide the service you requested — scanning, conversion, translation, or OCR — and for no other purpose.

Documents are retained for as long as your account remains active. You can delete individual documents at any time from the History screen within the app. You can delete your entire account and all associated data by contacting us at desolate1250@gmail.com.

**OCR processing is performed entirely on your device.** When you use the OCR Scanner, your camera image and the recognised text are processed locally using Google ML Kit. No image or raw camera data is transmitted to our servers or any third-party server during OCR.

| Service | Purpose | Privacy Policy |

When you request a translation, your text is transmitted to Google Cloud Translation. These transmissions are necessary to provide the service and are protected by HTTPS encryption.

---

## 5. Data Storage and Security

All data transmitted between the VeriScript app and our servers is encrypted using HTTPS/TLS. Your account credentials are managed through Supabase Auth with industry-standard security practices.

API keys and service credentials are never stored in the app itself — they are kept securely in server-side environments and never exposed to your device.

Your data is stored on Supabase servers. Supabase uses Row Level Security (RLS) to ensure that your data is only accessible by you — no other VeriScript user can access your documents, scan reports, translations, or personal information.

While we take reasonable steps to protect your data, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.
